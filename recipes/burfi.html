Description

Barfi, barfee, borfi or burfi is a dense milk-based sweet from the Indian subcontinent. The name comes from the Hindustani word barf, which means snow.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Coconut
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Dori me
  2. Interimo ayapare, dorime
  3. Latire, latiremo
  4. Dori me
